About Me

Accomplished Associate Professor and researcher specializing in environmental policy, energy economics, and sustainable development. With a strong background in quantitative analysis and a passion for addressing climate risk, I lead research and teach at the intersection of economics and environmental science. My work focuses on clean energy transitions, corporate ESG behavior, and low-carbon policy evaluation.
